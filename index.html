Lastest Project

Pokimon

This website is a fun and informative tool for Pokemon fans! Enter the name of any Pokemon in the search bar and click the "Get Pokemon Information" button. The website will then display the Pokemon's image, type, stats, and top 4 strongest moves! The background of the Pokemon's information card will even change color to match the Pokemon's type!.

Weather App

This web app helps you plan your camping trip by showing the current weather at your chosen campground. Enter the campground name and state, and the app uses Google Maps and a weather API to find the location and display temperature, wind speed, and precipitation. With this information, you can pack accordingly for a comfortable camping adventure!
